How SmartEduLoan Works

Three simple steps to get your education funded.

📝

1. Build Your Credit Profile

Enter your academic scores, upload documents (Aadhar, PAN, marksheets), and our algorithm calculates your credit score — giving you transparent, fair eligibility.

🏦

2. Choose a Bank

Browse partner bank products, compare interest rates, and submit your loan application in minutes.

⚡

3. Get Approved Fast

Admin reviews your application, bank verifies your documents, and funds are disbursed — usually within 48 hours.
